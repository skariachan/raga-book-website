About

James Skaria is a Carnatic music rasikan who began his Carnatic journey in 2024 under the guidance of his guru Premji K Bhasi.

An enthusiastic learner with a deep curiosity for music, he is especially fascinated by rāgas—their structure, personality, and the subtle magic through which they communicate emotion and meaning.

This space is a personal attempt to explore, document, and reflect on rāgas as living musical entities rather than mere scales.
