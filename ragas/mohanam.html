MŌHANAM

Pentatonic rāga of brightness and purity

Rāga Structure

Ārohaṇa: S R₂ G₃ P D₂ S

Avarohaṇa: S D₂ P G₃ R₂ S

Jāti: Audava–Audava

Varjya Swaras: M, N

Rāga Lakṣaṇam

Mōhanam derives its beauty from simplicity and symmetry. With only five swaras, it creates a bright and expansive soundscape.

Bhāva

Joyful, auspicious, and calm. Mōhanam rewards restraint and clarity more than speed.

Personal Notes

Responds well when the mind is calm. Over-speeding reduces its depth.
